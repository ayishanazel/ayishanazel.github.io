AKI AT A GLANCE

AKI Building Materials Trading LLC, which offers first grade international sourced surface products and services for the architectural, wood working and general construction finishing materials market.
AKI is focused on Trading & Subcontract in UAE & GCC countries, the large-scale projects sector as well as the retails & distribution sectors within the furniture, interior design, architecture & Construction industries.
